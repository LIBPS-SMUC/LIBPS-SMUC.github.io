Overview of the SMUC System

The LIB Provisional Studio's Members and Users Congress (SMUC), established on February 18, 2025, serves as the primary decision-making body of LIB Provisional Studio, operating under the principle of democratic centralism. It convenes quarterly (typically in January, March, July, and October), with the Special Assembly (SASMUC) handling urgent matters when necessary. All resolutions are adopted through anonymous voting, with major decisions requiring approval by either the Joint Founders or a majority of members and user representatives.

This institution represents the fundamental mechanism through which all members and users participate in studio governance and determine significant affairs.

  • Representative Composition: Internally elected members + externally nominated users (based on contribution level).
  • Core Authority: Election of rotating chairpersons, formulation/revision of statutes, development planning, and decision-making on major matters.
  • Oversight Mechanism: The Emergency Committee of LIBPS may assume functions for up to 30 days to ensure rapid crisis response.
Note: This governance system is administered by the Committee of the Members and Users Congress.

Authority of the Members and Users Congress

1. Election of Rotating Chairperson

During each Congress's concluding session, a Rotating Chairperson shall be elected from studio management based on voting results. The Chairperson exercises defined executive powers and fulfills corresponding responsibilities.

2. Formulation and Revision of Studio Statutes

The concluding session holds authority to establish and amend fundamental guidelines and operational mechanisms, ensuring alignment with collective interests.

3. Development of Studio Strategic Plans

The Congress deliberates and decides on major initiatives, critical projects, and key personnel appointments to guarantee scientific, democratic, and impartial decision-making.

4. Determination of Major Studio Affairs

The concluding session discusses and resolves other significant matters essential for maintaining regular operations and sustainable development.
